achiever africa

The mentorship program of the AYLI is a way of fulfilling our vision of developing the youth demography of Africa by providing training and mentorship that bring about productivity and reduce brain drain in Africa. The mentorship program is open to youths between the age of thirteen and forty

mentorship focus

The mentorship program will focus in developing youth in the following areas

◆ Personal development

◆ Business development

◆ Skills enhancement

◆ Access to key players in various industries

◆ Creating opportunities for expression of talents

◆ Socio-cultural integration

◆ Gender inclusion platform

◆ Access to finance

Our Mission

Creating an enabling environment for African youths to assess inspiration, support and mentoring for the deployment of their creative ideas.

VISION

Our vision is to create a specified environment that promulgates intellectual discourse in the area of creatively channeling the drive of youth with ‘minded’ projects; showing them the part to achieve all they dream of and linking them with mentors in the industry of their destination
